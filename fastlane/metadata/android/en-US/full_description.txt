FLOSS Magnifier turns your phone into a comfortable magnifying glass, designed first for low-vision users.

Features:
- Live camera zoom with a large slider, big +/− buttons, and pinch
- Torch for dark or low-contrast reading
- Freeze the image, then pan and zoom the still — no more hand shake
- Tap to focus
- Large, high-contrast controls, full TalkBack support, haptic feedback
- English and French

Private by design: no network access, no ads, no analytics, nothing recorded or saved. Free software (AGPL-3.0), part of the Inspired Beings FLOSS collection.
