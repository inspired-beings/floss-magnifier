See clearly, share nothing. Fossling Magnifier turns your phone into a comfortable magnifying glass — designed first for low-vision users, handy for everyone.

🔍 ZOOM THAT WORKS FOR YOU
A large slider, big +/− buttons, and pinch to zoom — controls you can actually see and hit. Tap to focus.

💡 LIGHT UP THE SMALL PRINT
Flip on the torch for menus in dim restaurants, labels in cupboards, and low-contrast print.

🧊 FREEZE THE SHAKE
Capture a still, then pan and zoom it steadily. No more chasing text with a trembling hand.

♿ ACCESSIBILITY FIRST, ALWAYS
Large high-contrast controls, full TalkBack support, haptic feedback, and your system font size honored.

🔒 PRIVATE BY DESIGN
No network access, no ads, no analytics. Nothing is recorded or saved — the camera feed never leaves your screen.

🌍 IN YOUR LANGUAGE
Available in English and French.
